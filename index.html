How your deck score is calculated Each non-basic card gets a Card Uniqueness Score from 1 to 100 based on its EDHREC rank. U99 averages those scores together, then makes a small adjustment if the deck is especially staple-heavy or loaded with deep cuts.
How to raise your score Replace Commander Staples and Commander Favorites with Pet Cards or Unique Sleepers that still support your game plan.
